## Verified statistics

1. **WC2026 digital reach (FIFA primary source):** 20B video views across FIFA platforms, 30B impressions + 1.7B engagements on TikTok+YouTube, 187M unique FIFA.com visitors; +130% impressions / +160% engagement / +485% video views vs 2022. [FIFA press release](https://inside.fifa.com/media-releases/packed-stadiums-record-digital-reach-world-cup-2026-numbers-unprecedented-scale)
2. **Stadium attendance:** 6.25M spectators through Round of 16, 99.7% occupancy, avg 65,204/match. [Same FIFA release]
3. **US broadcast records:** USA–Bosnia 36.2M combined viewers (Fox+Telemundo); Mexico–England 36M (Televisa/Azteca); Spain–France semi 11.5M on Fox = most-watched semifinal in US English-language TV history. [FIFA release], [Variety](https://variety.com/2026/tv/news/spain-france-world-cup-ratings-breaks-record-1236812067/)
4. **Second-screen behavior:** 216.8M US adults (80.6%) will second-screen via smartphone in 2026; ~⅓ of fans purchase during live events; sports/live-score apps hit 137M downloads globally in Q2 2026 around WC kickoff. [MNTN Research](https://research.mountain.com/insights/digital-live-sports-audiences-are-projected-to-grow-5-8-in-2026/), [Sensor Tower](https://sensortower.com/blog/beyond-the-game-the-sports-report-2026)
5. **Common Goal (pledge-giving precedent in football):** members pledge ≥1% of earnings to a collective fund; 500+ stakeholders incl. 200+ pro athletes. [common-goal.org/one-percent](https://www.common-goal.org/one-percent). ⚠ The "90% projects / 10% admin" split is UNVERIFIED on the live page — do not use.
